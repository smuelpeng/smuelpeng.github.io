Biography

I am a first-year (2021-now) Ph.D. student in School of Electronic, Electrical, and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Qingming Huang (Professor at UCAS, IEEE Fellow). I have closely collaborated with Dr. Yichao Wu and Mr. Ding Liang. My research interests include machine learning and computer vision. I have done related research works on knowledge distillation, face recognition, dynamic neural network and learning with noisy label.

I received the B.E. degree in communication engineering and the M.E degree in electrical and communication engineering from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), Beijing, China, in 2015 and 2018, respectively.

I worked at SenseTime as senior researcher(2018-2021).
